Project 5: Pizza Sales Dashboard

Brief of Project: A detailed Power BI dashboard analyzing pizza sales performance, order trends, and product profitability across time slots, sizes, and categories.


Problem Statement: Built to extract actionable insights from a pizza sales dataset to help business optimize marketing, menu planning, and time-slot performance.

×

PIZZA SALES DASHBOARD

Project Overview: Created a comprehensive Power BI dashboard with interactive pages and tooltips to understand pizza sales, profit margins, order frequency, and top/bottom performers.

Dashboard Features (Page 1):

  • Visuals: Total Sales, Most Ordered Pizza, Profit, Bottom and Top 5 Pizza Sales, Quantity by Category
  • Graphs: Total Sales by Day Name, Total Sales by Day, Total Orders by Day Name.
  • Slicers: Category, Time Slot, Pizza Size, Pizza Name, Month
  • Tooltips: Total Orders by Time Slot , Total Sales by Time Slot

    • Dashboard Features (Page 2):

  • Total Sales by Category, Total Sales by Quarter
  • Total Quantity by Pizza Name
  • Table: Sales by Time Slot and Month (Afternoon, Evening, Morning, Night)
  • Decomposition Tree: Total Sales → Month → Time Slot → Pizza Name

    • Tools Used: Power BI, DAX, Power Query
